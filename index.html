Platform Features

Client Onboarding and Verification

Whether its an individual or a large family group, simple and intuitive forms guide clients through the onboarding and verification process.

Engagements and Payments

Streamline your engagement creation, approval and payment processing, with multiple acceptances, ensuring full compliance when engaging your clients.

Ethical Clearance (No Professional Objection)

Automate your ethical letter process by simplifying letter creation. The prior accountant can upload documents securely and will receive follow-up reminders automatically.

Security made simple

Mandatory SMS-based two-factor authentication secures client onboarding, while platform users benefit from Single Sign-On and authenticator app-based 2FA, to protect sensitive data at all times.

Make it your own

Tailor the onboarding, engagement, and ethical clearance experience, to showcase your brand with personalized logos and custom button colors.
