Engagements, Payments & Onboarding for Professional Services firms.

In today's competitive professional services landscape, managing client onboarding and engagement efficiently is crucial. However, juggling multiple systems for onboarding, engagements, and payments can create friction and inefficiencies.

OnboardMe unifies these essential workflows (and more) into a single, streamlined platform. Our solution simplifies client onboarding with intuitive forms, streamlines engagement processes with built-in approvals and ethical clearances, ensures secure + timely payments, all within one integrated system.

By eliminating the need for disparate tools, OnboardMe reduces complexity, enhances client satisfaction, and empowers your firm to focus on what truly matters: delivering exceptional service.

  • Consumption based banded pricing
  • Integration to Practice Management Systems
  • Built with ease of use in mind
  • Data securely hosted onshore using AWS

Platform Features

Client Onboarding and Verification

OnboardMe simplifies client onboarding and verification with intuitive, secure forms, that guide clients through the process seamlessly. The integrated validation and system integrations ensure accurate data every time.

Engagements and Payments

Streamline your engagements with our efficient workflows. From engagement creation to client approval and payment processing. OnboardMe supports multiple engagement acceptances, ensuring full compliance when engaging your clients.

Ethical Clearance

Simplify and streamline your Ethical Letter (No Profressional Objection) process, with automated reminders built into your engagement and onboarding experience. OnboardMe ensures that your peers receive timely reminders, and are able to upload documents to your Ethical Clearance requests, making the entire process seamless, secure and efficient.

2FA Security

Enhance security with mandatory two-factor authentication (2FA) during client onboarding. Firms can enable 2fa or Single Sign on options for their users, protecting sensitive client data at all times.

Custom Branding

Customize the onboarding, engagement and ethical clearance experience to reflect your brand, providing a consistent and professional image that builds trust and loyalty.
