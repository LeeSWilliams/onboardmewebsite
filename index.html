Simplified Engagement, Payment and Onboarding

In today's competitive professional services landscape, managing client relationships efficiently is crucial. However, juggling multiple systems for onboarding, engagements, and payments can create friction and inefficiencies.

OnboardMe unifies these essential workflows into a single, streamlined platform. Our solution simplifies client onboarding with intuitive forms, automates engagement processes with built-in approvals, and ensures secure, timely payments—all within one integrated system..

By eliminating the need for disparate tools, OnboardMe reduces complexity, enhances client satisfaction, and empowers your firm to focus on what truly matters: delivering exceptional service.

  • Consumption based pricing
  • Integration to Practice Management Systems
  • Built with ease of use in mind
  • Data securely hosted onshore using AWS

Platform Features

Onboarding

Simplify your Onboarding with our simple, secure, and intuitive onboarding forms, which allow your clients to provide you with the information that you need with automated follow ups.

Engagements

Simplify your engagement process with built-in re-engagement functionality including support for multiple engagement acceptances.

Payments

Recieve payment quickly and easily by Credit Card or Direct Debit, when Engagements are accepted.

Identity Verification

Client Verification during the Onboarding process means you can verify your client’s identity before commencing any work.

Client Onboarding

Our platform simplifies client onboarding with intuitive, secure forms that guide clients through the process seamlessly. The integrated validation and system integrations ensure accurate data every time.

Engagements and Payments

Streamline your engagements with our efficient workflow, from creation to client approval and payment processing. Our platform supports multiple engagement acceptances, ensuring a smooth transition to onboarding.

Multi-factor Secured Onboarding

Enhance security with two-factor authentication (2FA) during onboarding, protecting sensitive client data at all times in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

Branding

Customize the onboarding experience to reflect your brand, providing a consistent and professional image that builds client trust and loyalty.
